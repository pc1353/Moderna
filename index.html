Moderna Restaurant - Moderna Group

Founded by Chaniyara Pranav in 2003, the Moderna group is a global enterprise, comprising over 100 independently operating companies.

  • The Moderna brand stands as a lasting promise behind its businesses, many of which are industry leaders.
  • Moderna is honoured to be one of the first of those timeless and tireless initiatives.
  • The group operates in more than six continents with a mission to improve the quality of life of the communities it serves globally.

Our pivotal programme, Aspiration 2022, is based on the three-pronged strategy of re-structuring, re-engineering and re-imagining the Company’s portfolio. The strategy is aimed at margin enhancement and growth to fulfil our aspiration of becoming the most iconic and profitable hospitality company in South Asia. Therefore, it will focus on exploring new markets, expanding existing properties. The five-year programme will also include leveraging the digital channel to strengthen the brand and generate more revenue.
